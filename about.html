We Built FotoFinish to End the Guessing Game

We built FotoFinish because we lived the chaos of managing cleaning operations across dozens of sites and refused to accept that there was no better way.

The Problem We Lived

We Were Losing Our Minds Trying to Supervise at Scale

We were operators first. Before FotoFinish, we managed cleaning contracts across dozens of locations — retail chains, office buildings, healthcare facilities. Every day we faced the same impossible question: how do you know the work was actually done?

Supervisors were spending two hours driving to a site just to verify what took fifteen minutes to clean. We were flying blind between visits, trusting verbal reports, reacting to angry client calls about things that had been wrong for days. We were constantly behind, constantly apologizing, and constantly losing money on unnecessary travel.

We tried clipboards, WhatsApp groups, shared spreadsheets, hired more supervisors. Nothing scaled. Nothing gave us real-time proof. Nothing changed the behavior of the people doing the work.

The Turning Point

What If the Work Verified Itself?

One night, after a particularly brutal week of surprise client complaints and a supervisor who swore a site had been cleaned when it clearly hadn't, we asked ourselves: what would it take to make dishonesty nearly impossible and verification nearly effortless?

The answer wasn't more people. It was smarter technology. We designed a system where employees submit photo evidence at the end of every shift — guided by reference images that rotate randomly so no one can game it. The photos go straight to the dashboard. Supervisors see everything. Clients see everything. The guessing stops.

We called it FotoFlow. And it changed how we ran our own operations before we ever offered it to anyone else.

The Result

From Operators to Builders — and Back Again

We rebuilt our entire supervision model around FotoFlow. Supervisor travel dropped by more than half. Client satisfaction went up. Staff performance improved because accountability was visible every single day — not just on inspection days.

We realized we had solved a problem that every multi-site operator faces, whether they manage 5 sites or 5,000. So we built FotoFinish into a full platform, added the Evaluation Center, the Manager App, attendance tracking, and analytics — and opened it to the world.

Today, FotoFinish is trusted at over 6,000 sites across cleaning companies, facility management firms, franchises, retail chains, and more. We are still operators at heart. We built this for people like us — and we are not done building.
