Core Technology

FotoFlow: The Photo Verification Loop

A FotoFlow is the intelligence engine at the center of FotoFinish. Each FotoFlow is configured for a specific site or shift and contains a series of "Slots" — each slot holds multiple reference images of your site in ideal condition.

When an employee starts their report, the app randomly selects one photo from each slot and asks them to replicate it. This creates a daily-changing report of representative site photos that makes gaming the system virtually impossible.

  • Configurable slots for every area of your site
  • Random photo selection eliminates predictability
  • Automatic daily-changing reports delivered to supervisors
  • Patent-pending technology
