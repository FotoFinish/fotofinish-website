The Mobile App

Built for the Person Doing the Work

The FotoFinish app is designed to be used at the end of every shift — in a few taps, employees capture and submit their assigned photos, creating a verified record of every clean.

  • Side-by-side reference photos guide each capture
  • Randomly selected photos from each slot every shift
  • One tap to submit the completed report
  • View assigned tasks and corrective action items
  • Access full site history directly from the app
What the App Does

Everything On-site Users Need

Submit Photo Reports

Capture each assigned photo, replicate the reference, and submit your full shift report in minutes.

View Assigned Tasks

See any corrective action tickets assigned to you by your supervisor, with deadlines and context.

Access Site History

Review past photo submissions for your site and track improvement over time — all in the app.

For Supervisors

Managing Multiple Sites?

Supervisors and managers use the full web dashboard at app.fotofinish.com to review photo reports, track compliance across all sites, and issue corrective actions — from any device.

App Questions

Download the app from the App Store or Google Play, then log in with the credentials your account administrator has set up for you. Your assigned FotoFlows and sites will appear automatically.
The app itself is free to download. FotoFinish is a subscription service — your organization's administrator handles the subscription. On-site users and supervisors are included in the plan at no extra cost per user.
FotoFinish is available on iPhone (iOS 13 and above) and Android phones (Android 8.0 and above). The supervisor web dashboard works on any modern browser including Chrome, Safari, and Edge.
